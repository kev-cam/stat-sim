## Why a separate license is needed (two independent rights)

stat-sim implements methods covered by issued / pending patents of the author:

- **US 8,478,576 B1** — *Including Variability in Simulation of Logic Circuits*
  (probability-waveform method; the LEAD method).
- **US 2023/0334213 A1** — analog / mixed-signal defect-binning (DFX) method.

A commercial user needs **both**:

1. a **copyright** license to the source code (not granted for commercial use by
   the PolyForm Noncommercial License), and
2. a **patent** license to practice the patented methods commercially. The
   PolyForm patent grant is scoped to the noncommercial copyright grant, so it
   does **not** extend to commercial use. The patents are an independent right
   that applies even to an independent re-implementation of the methods.

The commercial license conveys both.
